About Us

JustFly is an airline based in India, which has long enjoyed a reputation for its comfortable cabins and attentive service. From humble beginnings, the airline has grown from operating with just two aircraft in 1985 to now flying the biggest fleets of Airbus A380s and Boeing 777s in the world. Today we are proud to tell you that JustFly is one of the few airlines in the world to fly an all wide-body fleet. It offers travel in Economy, Business and First Class, and is known for its complimentary services available in over 75 cities worldwide. JustFly can take you to over 161 destinations across Africa, the Americas, Asia, Europe, the Middle East and the Pacific.
